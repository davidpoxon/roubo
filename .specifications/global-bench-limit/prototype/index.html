Walkthrough notes

  • US-001 Set a cap in Settings · Benches. Toggle between Unlimited and Limit; numeric input is disabled in Unlimited mode.
  • US-002 Switch the scenario to at cap. The "+ New bench" button gains aria-disabled="true", its visual state, and the tooltip swaps to "Global bench limit reached. 5 of 5 benches in use."
  • US-003 The "Global benches" meter mirrors the per-project meter style. Visible only when a cap is set; the fill bar turns red at the cap.
  • US-004 Switch to lowered. Existing benches stay; meter overflows visually (capped at 100% fill, count shows 5 of 3); New bench is blocked until the count drops below 3.
  • US-005 Switch to No cap. The global meter disappears, the New bench button is always enabled, per-project tiles are unchanged.
